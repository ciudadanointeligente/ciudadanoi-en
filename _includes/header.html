We use technology to reveal and to participate in democracy, we work to relocate power from the few to the many.

Promote Transparency that favors the establishment of a culture of access to public information and the use of open data.

Promote Accountability to develop larger and better mechanisms that privilege the collective interest over the individual.

Strengthen Citizen Participation to promote effective collective action, consolidating a democracy that is more representative and deliberative.
